About

Kenya Hara

Kenya Hara is a graphic designer,one of the most interesting protagonists on the international scene who was born in 1958, Japan,Okayama Prefecture.


He perfectly combines minimalism, simplicity, and the potency of inactivity into his compositions because of his deep knowledge of aesthetics. His artistic journey is above traditional limitations and includes curatorial projects, product design, physical installations, and graphic design.His original thinking and viewpoint have opened up opportunities for ground-breaking exhibitions and innovative design ideas.


He expresses his deep knowledge of the core of design through his books and publications.He continues to be an inspiration and help shape the future of art and design with his steadfast dedication to the pursuit of beauty and sharp eye for the interaction between design and daily life.


He is an artist of immense talent and vision and recognized for his multidisciplinary approach.His works inspire thinking and desire viewers to interact with the space around items because of his constant dedication to the concepts of minimalism and the investigation of nothingness.


His installations, such the provocative "Ex-formation," push the boundaries of perception and emphasise the value of empty space. Hara's work as a curator, which seamlessly combines art, design, and sensory experiences, has resulted in ground-breaking exhibitions like "Haptic" and "Senseware," in addition to his artistic practice. He offers his profound views on the philosophical foundations of design and its influence on society through his prominent publications, such as "White," and his collaborations on books like "Eames: Beautiful Details."


Kenya Hara continues to influence the art world, motivating both aspiring artists and seasoned designers with her ever expanding body of work and natural ability to push the limits of visual expression.
